Recommended Learning Path

Month 1: Foundation

  • Listen to the Bansuri Bass Bhairav alap (Audio page) — focus on how individual notes are explored
  • Compare the Carnatic flute recording with the Hindustani style — notice the differences in ornamentation
  • Explore the 1969 historical recording — observe how the traditional style laid groundwork for today's artists

Month 2-3: Deeper Listening

  • Study Pt. Hariprasad Chaurasia's recital — pay attention to his meend (glides) and breath control
  • Listen to the jugalbandi series — notice how two instruments respond to each other
  • Start identifying raags by their characteristic phrases (pakad)

Month 4+: Active Practice

  • Try to reproduce phrases you hear on your own bansuri
  • Record yourself and compare with the masters
  • Explore more recordings on Internet Archive
