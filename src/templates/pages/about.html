About Sonora

Empowering musicians worldwide to understand, analyze, and master the art of scale detection through cutting-edge technology and ancient musical wisdom.

🎯 Our Mission

At Sonora, we believe that understanding musical scales is fundamental to mastering any musical instrument. Our mission is to bridge the gap between ancient Indian classical music traditions and modern artificial intelligence to create an intuitive tool that helps musicians of all levels recognize, learn, and perfect the scales they play.

We're dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of Indian classical music while making it accessible to the modern world through technology.

🎵 How Sonora Works

  1. Record or Upload: Play your flute or upload a recording of your performance.
  2. AI Analysis: Our advanced algorithms analyze the audio to identify frequency patterns and note sequences.
  3. Scale Recognition: The system matches patterns against 10 classical Indian Thaats to determine your scale.
  4. Detailed Results: Receive confidence scores, top candidates, and detailed frequency visualizations.
  5. Learn & Improve: Use insights to refine your technique and explore new scales.
